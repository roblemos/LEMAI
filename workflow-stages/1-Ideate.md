# I — Idea: Ideation & Brainstorming

 **LEMAI: AI-L2 [I1,D2,W3,R2,V1,F2]**  



The Idea stage covers initial concept generation, problem framing, and exploration of possibilities before any structure is committed. This is where intent, scope, and creative direction are established. In writing, it includes topic selection and research framing; in software development, it includes requirements discovery and solution exploration.

---

| Activity | AI Level | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Researching a topic using books, articles, and personal notes to form original ideas | 0 | No AI involved; all ideation is human-driven from primary sources |
| Asking AI to explain an unfamiliar technology or design concept encountered during requirements gathering | 1 | AI answers a targeted question; human drives all ideation and decides what the information means for the project |
| Using an AI chatbot to check whether a story angle has already been widely covered in recent publications | 1 | AI is used reactively as a lookup tool; the human drives and evaluates all ideas |
| Iteratively prompting AI to pressure-test a proposed technical approach and surface potential edge cases or risks | 2 | Human and AI refine the concept together; human directs the session and retains final judgment |
| Running a brainstorming session with AI to generate potential narrative angles or framing hooks, then selecting among them | 2 | AI contributes meaningfully to the option set, but the human chooses and refines |
| Asking AI to propose the primary technical approach and technology stack for a new project based on a brief description | 3 | AI generates the core concept; human reviews and may accept or adjust the output |
| Asking AI to propose the main narrative angle and structural approach for an article based on a topic brief | 3 | AI produces the ideation artifact; human role is evaluative rather than generative |
| AI agent monitors support tickets and usage telemetry and autonomously generates feature proposals for the product team | 4 | Human role is downstream approval only; AI drives all ideation end-to-end |
| AI system continuously scans news feeds and social trends, autonomously generating story pitches without prompting | 4 | Fully AI-native ideation; human involvement is limited to reviewing AI-produced concepts |
