# W — Work: Writing & Coding

 **LEMAI: AI-L2 [I1,D2,W3,R2,V1,F2]**  



The Work stage is where the primary artifact is produced — prose is written, code is authored, data pipelines are built. This is typically the most labor-intensive stage and the one where AI assistance is most visibly transformative. The level of AI involvement here has the greatest effect on the character of the output.

---

| Activity | AI Level | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Writing all copy or code manually without any AI assistance | 0 | Purely human execution; no AI tools involved at any point |
| Asking AI to explain why a specific error is occurring in code and how to fix it | 1 | AI answers a targeted diagnostic question; human writes all code and applies the fix |
| Using AI to suggest rephrasing for a sentence the human has already identified as weak | 1 | AI provides a reactive suggestion on a human-flagged issue; the human decides whether to apply it |
| Pair-coding with AI, where the human writes function signatures and logic stubs and AI fills in implementations for review | 2 | Human and AI co-produce the output; human shapes direction and edits throughout |
| Asking AI to draft a transitional paragraph or section introduction based on a detailed human-written outline point | 2 | AI accelerates execution on a well-specified task; human provides intent and reviews output |
| Using AI to draft a complete module or service based on a spec, with the human's role limited to reviewing and refining the output | 3 | AI produces the primary artifact; human's role is review and editing rather than authoring |
| Giving AI a reporting brief and having it draft the bulk of an article, with the human refining voice and verifying facts | 3 | AI drives the work phase; human validates output after production |
| AI agent receives a task ticket and autonomously writes, tests, and commits code | 4 | Human involvement is limited to task definition and final inspection; AI executes end-to-end |
| AI system generates and publishes content continuously based on configured parameters | 4 | Fully AI-native execution; no human involvement in the work phase itself |
