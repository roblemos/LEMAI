# F — Finalize: Formatting, Publishing & Deployment

 **LEMAI: AI-L2 [I1,D2,W3,R2,V1,F2]**  



The Finalization stage prepares the artifact for delivery and releases it to its audience or environment. In writing, this includes formatting, applying style guides, SEO optimization, and publishing. In software, it includes packaging, CI/CD pipeline execution, configuration management, and production deployment. Errors here can propagate at scale.

---

| Activity | AI Level | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| Manually formatting a document, configuring deployment scripts, and publishing by hand | 0 | Entirely human execution; no AI tools used at any step |
| Asking AI for the correct syntax for a specific deployment configuration or environment variable | 1 | AI answers a targeted configuration question; human applies and verifies the result |
| Using an AI style tool to flag formatting inconsistencies in a draft for manual review and correction | 1 | AI surfaces issues reactively; all finalization decisions are made by the human |
| Working with AI to scaffold a deployment pipeline, reviewing and approving each configuration step before it runs | 2 | Human and AI co-create the finalization process; human owns sign-off at each step |
| Using AI to apply a publication's house style guide across a draft, then manually reviewing every suggested change | 2 | AI executes a defined formatting task; human verifies all changes before accepting |
| Using an AI-managed CI/CD pipeline that runs tests and stages a release automatically, with the human approving the final deploy | 3 | AI handles execution; human retains the deployment decision but does not verify every step |
| Having AI automatically format, tag, and prepare a finished article for CMS publication, with the author doing a final spot-check | 3 | AI handles the bulk of finalization; human performs a high-level review rather than line-by-line |
| AI agent packages, tests, and deploys a new software release to production without any human approval step | 4 | Fully automated finalization; no human in the loop after the artifact is handed off |
| AI system automatically formats, schedules, and publishes content across channels without any human approval | 4 | Fully AI-native operations; human involvement is limited to defining high-level publishing policy |
